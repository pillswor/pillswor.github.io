    • Star Formation in Galactic Contexts

  • Interstellar medium

    • Structure Formation in the ISM

    I use a 0.5 kpc cube to analyze the formation of filamentary structure in the ISM and how that affects the clusters that form.

    • Formation of Star Clusters

    I investigate the formation of the most massive star clusters, and how they form massive stars.
